Made in US

Our tea station prides itself on offering teas that are blended and packaged in the United States, ensuring high standards of quality and supporting local businesses.

Relaxation

Our tea station features a range of herbal and green teas known for their calming properties. Ingredients like chamomile, lavender, and lemon balm are commonly used to promote relaxation and reduce stress.

Energy

The energy-boosting teas are designed to provide a balanced increase in energy without the jitters often associated with coffee, making them ideal for a morning kickstart or an afternoon pick-me-up.

Family Recipie

Some of the teas at our station are based on cherished family recipes passed down through generations. These blends carry a sense of tradition and history, offering a unique taste that connects drinkers to the past.

Expresso

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee that is brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans. This method produces a rich, full-bodied coffee with a thicker consistency and a more intense flavor compared to regular drip coffee.

Americano
Rs. 250

An Americano, also known as Caffè Americano, is a popular coffee drink made by diluting one or more shots of espresso with hot water. This results in a beverage that has a similar strength but different flavor profile compared to traditionally brewed coffee

Latte
Rs. 100

A latte, or caffè latte, is a popular espresso-based coffee drink that combines espresso with steamed milk and a small amount of milk foam. The latte is known for its creamy texture and smooth flavor, making it a favorite among coffee enthusiasts worldwide.

Original Coffee
Rs. 50

The term "original coffee" can refer to coffee in its most traditional and unaltered form. This usually means coffee made from freshly ground beans, brewed without additional flavorings, syrups, or significant alterations

About our

Tea Station

Our tea station is a curated collection of high-quality teas designed to provide a variety of experiences, from relaxation to energy boosts. Emphasizing American craftsmanship and family heritage, your tea station stands out for its unique blend of tradition and innovation.

Our tea station is committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging, supporting fair trade practices, and sourcing organic ingredients whenever possible. By supporting local farmers and suppliers, your tea station fosters a sense of community and contributes to the local economy.
